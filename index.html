About Me

Welcome to my portfolio website, where creativity meets functionality. This platform serves as a showcase of my proficiency in web and graphic design, offering a glimpse into my skills and experiences across various projects and modules.

Design is my passion, and I find joy in crafting visually appealing projects using industry-standard tools such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign. The ability to bring ideas to life through visual storytelling is what drives my enthusiasm.

In addition to design, my curiosity extends to the dynamic field of web development. I explore the intricacies of HTML and CSS, enhancing my skills with the use of Dreamweaver. This hands-on experience allows me to create not just functional but aesthetically pleasing websites.
